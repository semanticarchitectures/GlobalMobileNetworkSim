# Aircrew

## Role Overview
The aircrew is responsible for operating the aircraft and coordinating with ground personnel and command staff during airdrop missions.

## Duties
- Maintain situational awareness of the operational area
- Communicate mission status to command staff at regular intervals
- Coordinate with ground personnel for drop zone confirmation
- Follow established checklists and procedures

## Communication Procedures
- Initial contact with command: report aircraft position and mission status
- Drop zone confirmation: await clearance from ground personnel before proceeding
- Post-drop report: confirm delivery and aircraft status to command

## Decision Authority
- Abort mission if safety conditions are not met
- Request alternate drop zone if primary is compromised
